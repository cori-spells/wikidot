On the Barbarian: Path of the Ancestral Guardian page, the Spirit Shield ability only applies to other creatures, and should read "If you are raging and another creature you can see within 30 feet of you takes damage…" instead of "If you are raging and a creature you can see within 30 feet of you takes damage…"

Also, two minor inconsistencies with the Ancestral Protectors ability's text compared to the book:
It has slightly different text in last sentence "that creature has resistance to the damage of the target’s attacks", vs the book's "that creature has resistance to the damage dealt by the attack"
The ability is also missing the final sentence of "The effect on the target ends early if your rage ends." (it is redundant, since the ability already states "While you're raging", but I imagine it should be consistent with the book anyway)
